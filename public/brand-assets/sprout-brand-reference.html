04 — Design System Notes

MARK CONCEPT
Two asymmetric leaves emerge from a shared stem — one slightly recessed to suggest depth without gradient. Concentric rings below the stem evoke a haptic tap pulse, grounding the growth metaphor in interaction.

COLOR
Forest Green #1A4A2E · Off-White #F4F0E8
Single ink value. No mid-tones. The off-white fill on dark green preserves warmth at small sizes.

TYPEFACE
Playfair Display — the serif weight adds authority while remaining approachable. Mono captions keep system language grounded.

USAGE RULES
— Never recolor the mark mid-green or teal
— Minimum app icon: 29 × 29 px
— Minimum standalone mark: 24 × 24 px
— Minimum clearspace: ½ mark height
— Dark background: use cream (#F4F0E8) fill
— Light background: use forest green (#1A4A2E) fill

HAPTIC RING USAGE
The tap-pulse rings appear only at 96px and above. At smaller sizes, drop them to preserve clarity of the core sprout mark.
